about me
i'm a software engineer with a deep love for low-level systems and game development. i've been building with Unity & C# for about five years, and have spent the last few years diving into backend systems, game networking, and developer tooling.

recently, me and a few friends decided to build our own studio together--Ariworks. we love the little things, and love what we do.

right now i'm pouring most of my energy into Limeko — an open-source game engine — because i believe devs deserve tools built with love. when i'm not writing engine code, i'm probably somewhere quiet, wrapped up and thinking too hard about something. ✦

a few things worth knowing about me, if we end up working together or just talking: i struggle with anxiety, and there are days where i go quiet or need more space than usual — it's not personal, i just get overwhelmed sometimes. i'm also sensitive to heavy or hostile topics like politics, discrimination, and that whole category of things. they hit me harder than they probably should, and can knock me off my feet for a while.

i'm pretty complex to be close to. when i trust someone, i open up a lot — maybe too much — and i can start reading into things even when i know they're not meant harshly. patience and gentleness go a long way with me. i'm worth it, i promise. i just need someone who's okay with waiting.
