Limeko is a custom-built game engine designed to give you your favorite features, while also pushing to be the best game engine available. Not for ego--for love of the game.

Core Systems

OpenGL Rendering

A Modern Rendering Pipeline with materials, lighting, and framebuffer workflows, built around OpenGL.

Bepu Physics

High-performance physics integration with deterministic simulation and custom entity bindings.

Entity Architecture

Flexible component-driven design built for experimentation and rapid iteration.

Engine Tools

Debug visualization, runtime inspection, and systems designed to grow alongside development.
