Features

Rendering

One Rendering-Pipeline for every need. Just flick a toggle, or find a community-made plugin.

Physics

Fast, deterministic Rigidbody simulations wired directly into Entities.

Powered by Bepu Physics!

Entities

A Flexible component architecture designed for fast iteration and streamlined control.

Tooling

Debugging, Fixing, Iterating, Creating, and Developing, all given unique tools to make it easier.

Community

Ideas, feedback, and lots of weird suggestions slowly shaping Limeko.

And more...

All in one Free, Open-Source program.
