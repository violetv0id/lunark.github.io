Early Development

Limeko is actively in development. Many visuals, examples, and even parts of this website are experimental or temporary. This page exists to document progress, explore presentation ideas, and share the direction the engine is heading — not to present a finished product.

Some elements may be rough, strange, or intentionally playful. The goal is simple: build something ambitious first, then refine it into something exceptional.

Built With Intent

Limeko is a custom-built game engine created from the ground up to understand, explore, and rethink how modern games are made. Every system is designed with clarity, experimentation, and long-term growth in mind — prioritizing learning, control, and developer freedom over shortcuts.

Features

Custom Rendering Pipeline

A modern rendering pipeline featuring materials, lighting workflows, and framebuffer-driven rendering designed for extensibility, and support.

Bepu Physics

High-performance deterministic simulation powered by Bepu Physics, paired with custom entity bindings for engine-level control.

Entity Architecture

A flexible, component-driven architecture made to encourage rapid experimentation and iterative design.

Engine Tooling

Debug visualization, runtime inspection tools, and developer-focused workflows that evolve alongside the engine itself.

Community-Driven Direction

Limeko grows through experimentation, feedback, and iteration. Today’s bad design are tomorrow's inside jokes.
